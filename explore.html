ABOUT US

GYMBISON was founded on May 2nd, 2026, in St. George, Utah. Our purpose is to guide those who are in the gym or not, with a community available to anyone around the world. We provide high tier equipment for the best and workout guidance for our community. Our idea of GYMBISON was sparked the day we stepped foot in the gym, to spread love and discipline to those who are either in our community or not.

The Team

The founders of GYMBISON all grew connections in the gym. We spend everyday thinking of new ideas, testing each and every fabric, and helping every single member of our community. We started working out at different points of our lives, but that didn't stop us from joining together. JOIN THE HERD.
